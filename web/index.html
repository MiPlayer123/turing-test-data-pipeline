Two signals give them away.

We measured every conversation for these. One you just saw — the red one.

Hedging

"I think", "maybe", "sort of" — words that signal uncertainty. Humans use them naturally. AIs use them when they're trying to seem human.

H–H
maybe we go? yeah I think so
H–AI
Um, so I suppose… perhaps that works
AI–AI
Um.. maybe… I think probably

Repetitiveness

Repeating the same phrases back. A small tell, but a real one — AIs mirror themselves more than humans do.

H–H
I feel like yeah yeah I feel like
H–AI
makes sense yeah yeah makes sense
AI–AI
maybe… yeah maybe I think… I think

The baseline.

Now add AI.

AIs alone together.
