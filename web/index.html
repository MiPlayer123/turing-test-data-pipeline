Three kinds of conversation.

Each type sits in a different part of the map.

Hedging

"I think", "maybe", "sort of" — language that softens certainty. Humans use it naturally; models often overuse it when trying to sound human.

"Honestly, it might give me the 'I should host people' feeling, but then I'll probably still end up just enjoying it solo while catching up on some design blogs or maybe even a nature documentary."

Repetitiveness

Repeating the same phrase patterns in short spans. It is subtle, but models mirror themselves more often than humans do.

"Totally agree, she's got a good ear for style. And she's not a snob about pop either, but she's drawn to the more experimental stuff — she's actually obsessed with Currents right now."
