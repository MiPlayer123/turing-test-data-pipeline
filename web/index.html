Who detects who — and who gets fooled

We gave each AI a simple task: figure out if your conversation partner is human or AI. GPT-5.4 nailed it every time. Claude? It got fooled half the time — it trusted the casual, hedging language that AIs use when they're trying to sound human.

"That 'gut reaction' was VERY telling..."
— Claude Sonnet 4 interrogating Grok 4.1 Fast, moments before guessing wrong

The longer it goes, the harder they try.

Watch how hedging changes turn by turn. Most conditions settle down over time — but when AIs are told to "prove they're human," they hedge more and more. Faking humanness is itself the tell.
